Chapter One

The Brotherhood

We came to Namilyango as boys in 1990. We left as men in 1996. What was forged in those six years has outlasted decades.

Ngobus '90 is the collective of those who walked those halls together — through the boarding houses, the chapel mornings, the rugby pitches, the dining halls, the prefect appointments, the leavers' prom at Nabisunsa. Our teachers shaped us. Our rivals tested us. Our friendships sustained us. Thirty years on, we remain bound by something neither time nor distance has unstitched.

This is our digital home. A place to see the old pictures, honour the comrades who have gone before us, track the investment club, register for insurance plans, and keep the brotherhood's fire lit — not as nostalgia, but as a living, breathing force.

N.I.C.E.

NICE
Ngobus Investment Club Endeavour

Not every brother is in N.I.C.E. Not every brother needs to be. But for those who choose to pool their capital, their judgment, and their patience — this is the vehicle through which Ngobus '90 builds wealth, together.

Reports are published. Projects are visible. The chairman and treasurer communicate openly. If you are not yet in N.I.C.E., this section shows you exactly what you are missing — and why you might want in.
